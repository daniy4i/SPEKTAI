02 // Problem Statement

The waste we're eliminating.

The average knowledge worker loses 3.2 hours per day to communication overhead: composing follow-up emails, manually logging meeting notes, tracking action items across apps, and re-explaining context to multiple tools. That's 40% of the workday — gone.

Existing AI assistants require a screen. They require typing. They require an internet connection, an app install, a login, and often a subscription decision before value is demonstrated. They fail the fundamental test: you shouldn't need to think about the tool while you're thinking about your problem.

Phone calls remain the highest-bandwidth communication channel in existence. They carry tone, urgency, nuance, and real-time decision-making. Yet they are entirely ephemeral — nothing is captured, nothing is structured, nothing is acted on automatically.

Pain Point 01

Post-call amnesia

52% of action items from verbal conversations are never completed. No capture = no execution.

Pain Point 02

Context switching tax

Workers switch between 9.4 apps per day. Each switch costs 23 minutes of refocus time. (Gloria Mark, UC Irvine)

Pain Point 03

AI adoption friction

74% of AI tool trials are abandoned within 7 days due to setup friction. Onboarding is the enemy.

03 // Solution Overview

One number. Total intelligence.

SPEKT AI removes every layer of friction between a person and their AI. The product is a phone number. That's the entire interface. Call it. Talk. Hang up. SPEKT does the rest.

The Four Pillars

  • Surgical Minimalism. One input, perfected. Like the iPod click wheel — the interface disappears. The call IS the product.
  • Engineered Luxury. Every detail serves a function but the craft makes it beautiful. Processing times under 4 seconds. Zero dropped data.
  • Collective Identity. 800M people, each with their own AI, all on one network. The i-Signal. Personal but universal.
  • Total Memory. Persistent context across every call. SPEKT remembers what you said last Tuesday. It connects the dots you can't.
What SPEKT does after every call
  • Transcribes full conversation (Whisper, <2s)
  • Extracts action items with deadlines and priority
  • Surfaces key outcomes and decisions made
  • Updates persistent memory with new facts about you
  • Adjusts AI tone and style to match your preferences
  • Syncs to iOS app, Calendar, Reminders, and Contacts
  • Notifies you when the briefing is ready
04 // Product Description

The full product surface.

iOS Companion App

The iOS app is the intelligence dashboard — not the interface. Users never need it to invoke SPEKT. They use it to review, edit, and manage what SPEKT captured from their calls.

  • Voice Screen — One-tap dial with ambient waveform visualizer
  • Activity Feed — Chronological outcome timeline, grouped by date
  • Signal Screen — Persistent AI identity: memories, patterns, preferences
  • Processing View — Live pipeline status with staggered result reveal
  • Tasks — Full task manager synced from call extractions
  • Profile — Integrations, permissions, subscription management
The Signal (AI Identity)

Every user has an i-Signal — their unique AI identity layer. It grows with every call, storing memories, learning communication preferences, and personalizing responses over time.

  • Pinned + searchable memory bank
  • Voice tone preference learning (direct, formal, casual)
  • Response format preferences (bullets, prose, brief)
  • Usage pattern analysis (peak call times, topics, duration)
  • Bulk memory management with pin-all and clear actions
  • Context menu: edit, pin, delete per memory

Backend Stack

  • AI: OpenAI Whisper (transcription) + GPT-4o (extraction)

  • Deploy: Railway (Nixpacks, auto-deploy from GitHub)
